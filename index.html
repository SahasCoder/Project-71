Welcome Message


Soundarya Educational Trust (R) has a glorious history. Trust started Soundarya Central School in the year 2014 with the aim to provide holistic education that enables growth, optimism, hard work and all round development with a better understanding of the cultural, moral and social values of one’s life. The school is affiliated to the CBSE Board. Today it boasts of being one of the best educational institutions in North Bangalore nurturing and moulding the young minds and talents. The 6 -acre school campus has around 1200 students in the sixth year of its operation. The Ground + 6 floor building and playground with greenery all around, the school campus is designed to cater to the needs of young, growing children with most modern facilities. The school believes in awakening the young minds to think: “Where they are and Where they should go” The Soundaryans strive in an environment that fosters along with academic achievements, personal growth and development assisted by a team of well qualified and devoted faculty members with the resources that assist on their journey to become an excellent human being.
